Frequently Asked Questions

❓ How do I find a tutor?

Simply browse through our list of verified tutors, check their qualifications and reviews, and contact them directly via WhatsApp or our contact form.

❓ Are your tutors verified?

Yes! All tutors go through a rigorous verification process including background checks, qualification verification, and reference checks.

❓ How can I become a tutor?

Click on "Register as Tutor" and fill out the registration form. Once approved, your profile will be visible to parents looking for tutors.

❓ What subjects are available?

We have tutors for Mathematics, Science, English, Hindi, Sanskrit, Social Studies, Computer Science, Urdu, and many more.

❓ Do you offer online tutoring?

Currently, we focus on home tuition where tutors visit students at their homes. Contact us to discuss specific requirements.

❓ What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Hyderabad and surrounding areas. Contact us to check availability in your specific location.
